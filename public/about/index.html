About

We are an student-led fashion show, aiming to raise money for local charities in the North East of England.

Founded in 2020, John Snow Chairty Fashion aims to empower the student body of John Snow College, all whilst raising money for our local community.
Having successfully navigated the Covid-19 Pandemic in its second year, raising over £2000 for SafeLives, JSCFS went on to raise over £7500 for Solidaritee in 2022, a student-led refugee charity.
2022 saw JSCFS think bigger, laying the foundations for years to come.
2023 built upon its predecessors, striving to never be stationary. Raising over £13000 for a local youth homelessness charity, Moving On Durham, the show grasped a new audience and new goals.
JSCFS 2024 aims to be the best yet. Thinking bigger, more dynamically and more progressively. The show has aimed to break down barriers for accessibility, inclusion and equality. With a larger, more engaged audience, JSCFS 2024 aims to raise a record breaking amount for the this years charity, The Halo Project.
